SOUND :: PLAYSOUND

Play an 8-bit, WAV sample one time. 
SUB PLAYSOUND(
    label
)
Parameters
label - the address of the sample imported with #SOUND
Return
None.
Remarks
Enables interrupts (if they were disabled) and begins playing a WAV sample. Once the sample is finished it will stop. Calling DISABLEINTERRUPTS will halt the music from playing, as will STOPSOUND. Only one sample may be playing at any given time.
See Also
DISABLEINTERRUPTS | ENABLEINTERRUPTS | STOPSOUND | TURNSOUNDON
