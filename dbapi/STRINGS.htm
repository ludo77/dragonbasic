STRINGS Subroutines & Functions

APPEND$ - Appends one string to another, returning a new string.
COMPARE - Compare two strings for equality.
HEX$ - Converts an integer to a string.
LEFT$ - Gets the left portion of a string.
LEN - Calculates the length of a string.
MID$ - Gets the middle portion of a string.
MOVE - Copies one string into another.
PRINT - Prints a string to a tiled background.
RESIZE - Resizes the length of a string.
RIGHT$ - Gets the right portion of a string.
STR$ - Converts an integer to a string.
