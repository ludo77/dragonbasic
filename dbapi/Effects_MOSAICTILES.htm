EFFECTS :: MOSAICTILES

Toggles whether a background should use the mosaic effect or not. 
SUB MOSAICTILES(
    bg,
    flag
)
Parameters
bg - the background layer (0-3)
flag - 0 to turn off the effect, anything else enables
Return
None.
Remarks
When in tiled modes (0-2), background layers 0, 1 and 2 can make use of the GBA's mosaic effect.
