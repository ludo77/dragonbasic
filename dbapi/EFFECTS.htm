EFFECTS Subroutines & Functions

FADEIN - Fades the screen from black to normal.
FADEOUT - Fades the screen to black.
MOSAIC - Sets the mosaic registers in the GBA.
MOSAICTILES - Toggles whether a background should use the mosaic effect or not.
SPRITEMOSAIC - Toggles whether a sprite should use the mosaic effect or not.
