SOUND :: PLAYMUSIC

Play a looping, 8-bit, WAV sample. 
SUB PLAYMUSIC(
    label
)
Parameters
label - the address of the sample imported with #SOUND
Return
None.
Remarks
Enables interrupts (if they were disabled) and begins playing a looping WAV sample. Calling DISABLEINTERRUPTS will halt the music from playing, as will STOPMUSIC. Only one music sample may be playing at any given time.
See Also
DISABLEINTERRUPTS | ENABLEINTERRUPTS | STOPMUSIC | TURNSOUNDON
