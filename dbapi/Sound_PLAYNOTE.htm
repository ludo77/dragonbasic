SOUND :: PLAYNOTE

Plays a single note. 
SUB PLAYNOTE(
    channel,
    length,
    frequency,
    wave_duty
)
Parameters
channel - the sound channel to play out of (1 or 2)
length - the duration of the note to play (0-64)
frequency - the frequency of the note
wave_duty - the wave duty cycle (WAVE_DUTY_12, WAVE_DUTY_25, WAVE_DUTY_50 or WAVE_DUTY_75)
Return
None.
Remarks
This function will play a single note out of one of two possible sound channels. If length is 0, the note will play forever, otherwise 64 is the longest duration possible. Modifying frequency and wave_duty will alter the sound emitted.
