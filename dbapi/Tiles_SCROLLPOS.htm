TILES :: SCROLLPOS

Scrolls a background layer. 
SUB SCROLLPOS(
    bg,
    x,
    y
)
Parameters
bg - the background layer (0-3)
x - the absolute number of pixels to scroll horizontally
y - the absolute number of pixels to scroll vertically
Return
None.
Remarks
After scrolling the background, the change will not take effect until the next UPDATETILES is called.
