INTERRUPTS Subroutines & Functions

DISABLEINTERRUPTS - Turns off all interrupts.
ENABLEINTERRUPTS - Turns on all interrupts.
ONHBLANK - Creates or halts a horizontal blank interrupt control.
ONKEY - Creates or halts a keypad interrupt control.
ONTIMER - Creates or halts a user-defined, timer interrupt.
ONVBLANK - Creates or halts a vertical blank interrupt control.
ONVCOUNT - Creates or halts a vertical counter interrupt control.
