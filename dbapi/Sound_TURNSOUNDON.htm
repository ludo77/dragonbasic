SOUND :: TURNSOUNDON

Enables all sounds. 
SUB TURNSOUNDON
Parameters
None.
Return
None.
Remarks
Call this routine at the beginning of your program to enable the sound chip on the GBA. If you do not call this, no sounds will play.
NOTE: Most emulators incorrectly start with sound hardware enabled. This is not the way the real hardware behaves. If you want to hear sound, you have to call TURNSOUNDON.
