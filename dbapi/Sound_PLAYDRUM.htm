SOUND :: PLAYDRUM

Emits white noise from sound channel 4. 
SUB PLAYDRUM(
    length,
    frequency,
    wave_step
)
Parameters
length - the duration of the note to play (0-64)
frequency - the frequency of the noise
wave_step - the step frequency of the noise
Return
None.
Remarks
This function will play a white noise out of sound channel 4. If length is 0, the noise will play forever, otherwise 64 is the longest duration possible. Modifying frequency and wave_step will alter the sound emitted.
