INTERRUPTS :: ENABLEINTERRUPTS

Turns on all interrupts. 
SUB ENABLEINTERRUPTS
Parameters
None.
Return
None.
Remarks
No interrupts will take effect until this subroutine is called. NOTE: PLAYSOUND and PLAYMUSIC will automatically enable interrupts!
